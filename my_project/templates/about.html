Our Story

A Passion for compassion

The inspiration for Bark & Brush came from my own rescue dog, Max. After a terrible experience at a busy, impersonal grooming chain, I saw how stressed and anxious he was. I knew there had to be a better way — a place where a dog's emotional well-being was as important as their physical appearance.

My goal was to build a space where every dog, no matter their temperament or breed, could feel safe and comfortable while getting the high-quality care they deserve. This vision of a calm, compassionate grooming experience is what led me to open Bark & Brush.

Our Philosophy

Compassionate Care, Beautiful Results

At Bark & Brush, our mission is to provide the highest quality grooming in a safe, stress-free, and loving environment. We believe that grooming is more than just a haircut—it's an essential part of a dog's health and happiness.

We use only high-quality, all-natural shampoos and conditioners, and we take our time with each dog, ensuring a calm and positive experience. Our goal is to build trust with your pet, so they genuinely look forward to their spa day with us.

Our Team

I am a Certified Master Groomer and have dedicated my career to understanding canine behavior and creating beautiful, breed-standard cuts.
